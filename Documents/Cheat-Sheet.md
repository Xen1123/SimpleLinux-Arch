## Data Commands
- DD
  - Used to write data bit-by-bit
    - sudo dd if=/path/to/your/file of=/dev/device
  - **Zeroing Out A Drive (Completely Erasing It)**
    - sudo dd if=/dev/zero of=/dev/device
